Coconut Chutney Receipe

Ingredients

  1. 1 cup coconut fresh/desiccated
  2. 1 tbsp peanuts roasted
  3. 1 tbsp roasted split gram
  4. 2 petals of onion
  5. 2 green chilli
  6. small piece tamarind
  7. 1/2 tsp salt
  8. 1/2 cup of hot water
  9. 2 tsp oil
  10. 1 tsp mustard
  11. 1 red chilli broken
  12. few curry leaves
    13.

Instructions

  • Firstly, in a blender take 1 cup of coconut.
  • Also, add 1 tbsp roasted peanuts and 1 tbsp split gram for hotel style texture.
  • Additionally, add 2 petals onion, 2 green chilli, small piece tamarind and 1/2 tsp salt.
  • Blend to a smooth or coarse paste adding 1/2 cup of water.
  • Now prepare the tempering by heating 2 tsp oil.
  • Once the oil turns hot add 1 tsp mustard, 1 red chilli and few curry leaves.
  • Allow to splutter and pour tempering over chutney.
  • Finally, serve hotel style dosa chutney with dosa or idli.
